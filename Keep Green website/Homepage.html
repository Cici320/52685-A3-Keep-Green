About us

We call on people to participate in activities to protect the environment and save the earth, and to contribute to a better living environment. This website will help you to understand the meaning of environmental protection and what you can do to protect the environment, and to keep an eye on what is happening on the Earth.

Why it is important

1、Protecting the environment is a conscious effort by human beings to protect natural resources and make reasonable use of them to prevent the natural environment from being polluted and damaged. 2、Protecting the environment is to do a good job of comprehensive management of the polluted and damaged environment in order to create an environment suitable for human life and work, to coordinate the relationship between human beings and nature, so that people can achieve the concept of living in harmony with nature. 3、Environmental protection is to study and prevent the deterioration of the natural environment due to human life, production and construction activities, and then seek to control, manage and eliminate the pollution and damage to the environment by various factors, and strive to improve the environment, beautify the environment, protect the environment, so that it can better adapt to human life and work needs.
